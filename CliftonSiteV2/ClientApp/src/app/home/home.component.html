Looking for more information about agility?

Have a look around our site to find out more. Get in touch with us if want to have a go.

We are friendly KC registered club based in South Bristol. We teach agility at all levels, from complete beginners up to champ level.

We welcome new members, both complete beginners and people with more experience. For beginners, we regularly have Beginner classes starting through out the year. If you have more experience, let us know and we can find a suitable training group for you join. Just goto our Membership page, fill out the form and we'll be in touch.

In co-operation with Severnside DAC, we run the Wraxall Agility Show which is one of the largest in the region.
